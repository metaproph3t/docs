Crypto is both the biggest scam ever created and a major unlock in human coordination.

Tokens let entrepreneurs do things they could never do before:
- Financially align their users
- Bootstrap network effects with network mining
- Get instant market feedback on their actions

But tokens are also used to extract capital from investors. [The standard "low float / high FDV"
playbook has made investors wary of buying tokens.](https://youtu.be/99XsVSN_doQ?si=tEc6WePzplboWaQ1)

---

Enter MetaDAO.

MetaDAO is a full-stack fundraising and governance platform where investors are protected
from the most common kinds of rugs. This enables entrepreneurs to raise more money and
attract higher-quality investors.
